STOP CLIMATE CHANGE NOW

Learn more about and take action against climate change!

Causes

Billionaires and giant corporations are actively contributing to climate change by using energy irresponsibly and polluting the atmosphere, while governments are not doing enough to fight combat climate change, causing the problem to continuously worsen. To learn more about the science behind why climate change is happening, visit this NASA website .

Consequences

Climate change is causing mass destruction across the globe. Aside from the natural environment being harmed, more and more people are being harmed as a result of extreme weather phenomena that are happening because of global warming, such as fires, heat waves, and hurricanes. To find out more about the effects of climate change, and why all these extreme weather phenomena are occurring, visit this other NASA website .
